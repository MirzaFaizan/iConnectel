Company



iCONNECT is a leading global IT and telecommunication service provider offering a complete range of Voice, SMS, Data Center, Information Security, Physical Security and professional services designed to meet the ever changing needs of Fixed and Mobile Operators, ISPs, OTTs, Media & Content Players, Application Service Providers and Multinational Corporations (MNCs).
